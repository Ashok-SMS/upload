Inception and Growth

Bankatlal Badruka College for Information Technology (BBCIT), named after in honour of the founder, was established in 1991, to impart instruction of excellence in the area of IT. The college has been able to carve a niche for itself in the academic world in a short period of time due to hard work, dedication and scholarship of staff.
